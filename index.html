Intro

Our musical group, comprised of Igor Leeck and Reece Casteel, make up the talented guitarist duo located in the classical school Founders Classical Academy of Lewisville. Similarly, we play the beautiful, clean-sounding, and all-around fantastic instrument, classical guitar.

We started playing together in the 2nd semester of our Sophomore year. Ever since then, we've constantly improved and helped each other to become more talented guitarists. Our individual skills of playing guitar would never have turned into what they have become today without the constant struggles we have gone through together.

Although we're still in our first year, we have already had many performances at our school, with many more coming up in the future!

Igor Leeck

Igor Leeck started to play guitar when he was only five yeras old. Ever since then, he's been slowly improving at his craft. When he met Reece, their friendship and comradery helped improve their guitar skills immensely.

With this partnership with Reece, Igor has been hard at work, learning some of the most difficult and intense pieces. Some of these impressive songs include Isaac Alb√©niz's Asturias. Because of Reece's experience as a performer for most of his life, he is able to teach Igor the most effective and enjoyable way to entertain a crowd.

There is more to Igor than his guitar playing, however, as he is similarly talented academically and socially. He is known for always obtaining the impressive A Honor Roll for every quarter, and he has even won 2 awards concenrning his skill as a history buff. He also loves to help the school with its affairs, even running for and winning the Historian office for his school's Student Council.

Reece Casteel

Bringing joy and happiness to people all over the world is what this young, fresh, multi-talented singer, songwriter, guitarist, gentleman, performer, and entertainer, Reece Casteel, was born to do! Ever since he picked up a microphone, he has been touring all over the state of Texas giving rich performances.

Humming tunes since infancy, his parents recognized that he may have inherited some of the artistic talents of his famous great-grandparents, Harry James and Big-Band singer, Louise Tobin. It was ultimately recognized that Reece had great pitch by his great-grandmother at the young age of 4.

Although a performer for most of his life, he has only recently started playing the guitar, and he has improved very quickly ever since he picked it up. Thanks to his partnership and teamwork with Igor, he has been able to heighten his guitar skills to an amazing level. He is able to perform very difficult yet beautiful pieces such as Paganini's 24th Caprice.
